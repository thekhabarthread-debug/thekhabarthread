Editorial Policy

The Khabar Thread is committed to independent, accurate, and responsible journalism.

Editorial Principles

  • Accuracy before speed.
  • Fact-based reporting.
  • Fairness and impartiality.
  • Editorial independence.
  • Transparency and accountability.

Verification

Every effort is made to verify information through reliable sources before publication.

Corrections

If an error is identified, we review it promptly and publish corrections wherever necessary.
