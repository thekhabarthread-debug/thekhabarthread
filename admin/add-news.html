Publishing Tips

  • Title 60-70 characters ke andar rakhein — search mein achha dikhta hai.
  • Summary short aur clear rakhein, ye card aur social preview mein dikhta hai.
  • Image landscape (16:9) ho to homepage par sabse achha lagta hai.
  • Publish karne ke baad "All News" mein jaake Featured / Breaking flag set kar sakte hain.
  • YouTube video dalne ke liye link ko article ke andar ek alag line par akela paste karein — text ke saath mila hua nahi. Publish hote hi wahan video embed ho jayega.
  • Image lagane ke liye article ke andar jahan chahiye wahan cursor rakhkar seedha paste (Ctrl+V) kar dein — image apne aap upload hokar wahin lag jayegi.
