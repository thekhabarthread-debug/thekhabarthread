About Us

Welcome to The Khabar Thread

Welcome to The Khabar Thread, an independent digital news platform dedicated to delivering accurate, timely, and trustworthy journalism.

At The Khabar Thread, we believe journalism is more than simply reporting events—it is about uncovering the stories that truly matter. Our mission is to shed light on the issues that often remain overlooked, amplify voices that deserve to be heard, and provide fact-based reporting that informs, empowers, and inspires society.

Our newsroom covers breaking news, national affairs, Uttar Pradesh, politics, business, technology, sports, entertainment, world news, and important local developments. Every story is carefully verified and published with accuracy, fairness, balance, and editorial responsibility.

We are committed to independent journalism guided by the principles of truth, transparency, accountability, and public interest. Every report published on The Khabar Thread is prepared with the goal of providing reliable information that helps readers make informed decisions.

Founded by Akash Sharma, The Khabar Thread was established with a vision to create a credible digital news platform that values facts over speculation and public interest over sensationalism.

Our Mission

To deliver credible, unbiased, and impactful journalism while highlighting stories that deserve attention, promoting transparency, and ensuring every voice has the opportunity to be heard.

Our Vision

To become one of India's most trusted digital news platforms by promoting responsible journalism, factual reporting, public awareness, and editorial excellence.

Our Core Values

  • Truth & Accuracy
  • Editorial Independence
  • Transparency & Accountability
  • Ethical Journalism
  • Public Interest First
  • Responsible & Fair Reporting
